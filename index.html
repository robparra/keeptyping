OUTSOURCING

Outsourcing is a technique of distributing some work functions to another person or company instead of getting it done in-house. Typically, you see big companies outsourcing their tasks to different organizations. But a project can be outsourced to a company or a single person. It’s often said that outsourcing is a time and cost saving tactic, with which an organization can focus on core competencies. Today, outsourcing market has substantially grown and it sums up close to 89 billion dollars, which is double the amount it was in the last decade. This stat shows that how greatly outsourcing has influenced the businesses.

Why outsourcing?

Outsourcing gives access to a talented pool of teams which you don’t have to manage personally. This eliminates the burden of training them to achieve your desired results. Also, team management is a time-consuming process whereas outsourcing gives you more time for yourself to work on core competencies.
